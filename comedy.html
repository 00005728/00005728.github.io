The films in this list will make you laugh. Who does not love laughing? I bet everyone loves laughing and so do you! Watch these movies and laugh!

The big sick

An awkward true story

Plot: Kumail is a Pakistani comic who meets an American graduate student named Emily at one of his stand-up shows. As their relationship blossoms, he soon becomes worried about what his traditional Muslim parents will think of her. When Emily suddenly comes down with an illness that leaves her in a coma, Kumail finds himself developing a bond with her deeply concerned mother and father.

Cast: Kumail Nanjiani | Zoe Kazan | Holly Hunter | Ray Romano | Anupam Kher | Zenobia Shroff | Adeel Akhtar | Bo Burnham

Genres: Comedy | Drama | Romance

Country: USA

Director: Michael Showalter

Release date: June 23rd | 2017

Lady Bird

Plot: Marion McPherson, a California nurse, works tirelessly to keep her family afloat after her husband loses his job. She also maintains a turbulent bond with a teenage daughter who is just like her -- loving, strong-willed and deeply opinionated.

Cast: Saoirse Ronan | Laurie Metcalf | Tracy Letts | Lucas Hedges | Timothée Chalamet | Beanie Feldstein | Lois Smith | Stephen Henderson

Genres: Comedy

Country: USA

Director: Greta Gerwig

Release date: November 3rd | 2017

Ingrid goes west

Plot: Following the death of her mother and a series of self-inflicted setbacks, young Ingrid Thorburn escapes a humdrum existence by moving out West to befriend her Instagram obsession, a Los Angeles socialite named Taylor Sloane. After a quick bond is forged between these unlikeliest of buddies, the facade begins to crack in both women's lives -- with comically malicious results.

Cast: Aubrey Plaza | Elizabeth Olsen | O'Shea Jackson Jr. | Wyatt Russell | Billy Magnussen | Pom Klementieff | Hannah Pearl Utt | Joseph Breen

Genres: Comedy | Drama

Country: USA

Director: Matt Spicer

Release date: August 11th | 2017

Literally, Right Before Aaron

Plot: Adam is devastated to learn that his ex-girlfriend has invited him to her upcoming wedding. Returning home for the festivities, Adam must confront unresolved feelings while trying to convince himself and everyone else that he is truly happy for her.

Cast: Justin Long | Cobie Smulders | Ryan Hansen | John Cho | Kristen Schaal | Dana Delany | Peter Gallagher | Lea Thompson

Genres: Comedy | Drama

Country: USA

Directors: Ryan Eggold

Release date: September 29th | 2017

American-Made

Plot: Barry Seal, a TWA pilot, is recruited by the CIA to provide reconnaissance on the burgeoning communist threat in Central America and soon finds himself in charge of one of the biggest covert CIA operations in the history of the United States. The operation spawns the birth of the Medellin cartel and almost brings down the Reagan White House.

Cast: Tom Cruise | Domhnall Gleeson | Sarah Wright | Jesse Plemons | Caleb Landry Jones | Jayma Mays | Lola Kirke | E. Roger Mitchell

Genres: Action | Biography | Comedy | Crime | Drama | History | Thriller

Country: USA

Director: Doug Liman

Release date: September 29th | 2017
